8 Slider Puzzle
Starting the game:
	Select a image or upload your own!
Playing the game:
	Click on the image pieces to move them up,down,left, or right.
	Unscrambled the picture to get it back to its original image.
	Go fast! You are timed.
Extra Features:
	Hint: Moves 3 pieces to get you closer to finishing
	Pause: Pauses the game and stops the timer
	Undo: Undos your last moves
